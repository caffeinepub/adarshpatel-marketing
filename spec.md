# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Build a multi-section marketing website for "Adarshpatel Marketing" covering dairy feed products, agri inputs, and coffee trading, with a contact/enquiry form backed by a Motoko canister.

**Planned changes:**
- Sticky navbar with brand name "Adarshpatel Marketing" and section links (Home, Dairy Feed, Agri Inputs, Coffee Trading, Contact); collapses to hamburger on mobile
- Hero section with full-width agriculture-themed background image, company name, tagline covering all three business areas, and a CTA button linking to the products section
- Dairy Feed Products section with at least 3 product cards (name, description, "Enquire Now" button) using the dairy-feed background image
- Agri Inputs section with at least 3 product cards (fertilizers, pesticides, seeds, etc.) using the agri-inputs background image
- Coffee Trading section with 2–3 coffee product/service cards using the coffee background image
- Contact/Enquiry section with a form (Name, Phone Number, Product of Interest dropdown, Message) that validates fields and submits to the backend
- Motoko backend canister storing submitted enquiries; success confirmation shown after submission
- Earthy color palette: warm greens, soil browns, coffee browns, cream/off-white tones; bold heading typography; responsive layout; no blue or purple

**User-visible outcome:** Visitors can browse Adarshpatel Marketing's three product/service areas and submit an enquiry through a contact form, with all enquiries stored on-chain.
